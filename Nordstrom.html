Nordstrom Internship 2013

Wedding Stylist Feature for the Nordstrom iPhone App

Challenge

Nordstrom customers can only book a stylist appoint for the Wedding Suite online. In addition, >60% of Nordstrom customers own and use iPhones. There is an opportunity to bring the Wedding Suite features from Desktop to the Nordstrom Mobile App.

This independent project was the culmination of a summer long internship at Nordstrom Corporate in Seattle, WA. The goal of this project was to scale the wedding stylist booking feature from the Desktop to Mobile platform. Additionally features from the Wedding Suite online experience were added to the app as time allowed. I brought this app to a med-fi prototype stage before passing it off to the Nordstrom UX team.

Target Users

This app is specifically designed for current Nordstrom customers whom identify as female and are engaged & planning a wedding. These customers look to pinterest for inspiration and are familiar with the Nordstrom stylist program.

Sketches

The original design was determined by the styling on the desktop site and by following iPhone style guides. Additionally, some inspiration was taken from Pinterest since much of the target audience frequents it.

Guerilla User Research

To test my sketches, I conducted guerilla research with customers shopping in the Wedding Suite at the Seattle flagship store. I asked willing customers to describe what they thought specific features were on my sketches and I ran them through a scenario of booking a wedding stylist. I used the data from this experience to make my medium fidelity prototype.

In-Store Testing

Medium Fidelity Prototype

My final prototype was made in Axure. I included additional features such as a dress style guide and inspiration boards. Due to limited time, I did a final round of testing with my fellow interns.

Home Screen

While I did not have access to the Nordstrom style guide, I attempted to recreate the same feel as other features on the mobile app.



Appt Type

While the main function of stylist booking is for wedding dress shopping, the Wedding Suite offers many other appointment types. I attempted to display all different appointments using iconography and a grid system.
