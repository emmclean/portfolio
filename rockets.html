For our class project, we created a concept website for ESS 472: Rockets and Instrumentation. The website can serve as a portal for students, archive of projects for the public to view, and a launching point for companies and sponsors to get involved.

Sketches

We initially did a competitive analysis of other academic and lab websites, pulling features that we liked into our sketches. Our main goals for the site were to reduce cognitive load & present the large amounts of information in visually interesting ways, and to keep the website design academic.
