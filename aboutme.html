Hi, I'm Erin!

I'm an User Experience & Interaction Designer in Seattle. Currently I work for Walt Disney Parks and Resorts Digital as an Interaction Designer.
I graduated from the University of Washington with a degree in Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) and minors in Mathematics and Earth & Space Science. I have also studied at the University of Edinburgh's Schools of Informatics, Mathematics, and Engineering Management.

I have spent many summers up in the San Juan islands teaching leadership and facilitating challenge courses. In my free time, I can be found camping, hiking, and launching rockets.

I truly enjoy collaborating on large, challenging projects that are on the bleeding edge of new technology, especially in the fields of space sciences and bioastronautics.
